About Dr. Vinay

Meet the visionary behind Pragathi Dental Care

Dr. Vinay Kumar N S

BDS, F.A.G.E in Manipal, Oral Impalantology, Ortho Dentist at Bombay

With Over 12 years of experience in advanced cosmetic and restorative dentistry,
Dr. Vinay is a renowned dental surgeon dedicated to redefining luxury dental care in Karnataka. He completed his BDS from Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences and pursued a specialization in Aesthetic, Implant Dentistry (MDS) & Oral Impalantology.
